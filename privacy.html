Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

TrackHUD respects your privacy. Our core functionality relies on Apple Music integration and Apple Location Services to track your drives and logged songs.

2. Location Data

Location data is used locally on your device to contextualize your trips and playback events. We do not transmit this data to external servers or third parties.

3. Apple Music Integration

TrackHUD connects to your Apple Music account to observe currently playing music and build song statistics. This requires permission to access your media library. Your listening history and song data are solely used to generate personal insights within the app.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
