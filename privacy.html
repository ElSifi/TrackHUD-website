Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 27, 2026

1. Information We Collect

TrackHUD may collect the following types of information depending on how you use the app:

  • Basic usage data necessary for the app to function
  • Anonymized analytics data to help us improve the app
  • Location data when you use location-based features
TrackHUD respects your privacy and relies on Apple Music integration and Apple Location Services to track your drives and logged songs.

2. Analytics

We use third-party analytics tools (such as Firebase Analytics or Google Analytics) to understand how the app is used. These services may collect device identifiers, usage patterns, and crash reports. This data is used solely to improve the app experience. Please refer to the privacy policies of these services for more information.

3. Location Data

TrackHUD may access your device's location to provide location-based features. Location data is used locally on your device to contextualize your trips and playback events, and is only used while you are actively using the app. We do not transmit this data to external servers or third parties. Location data is never sold or shared except as required to deliver the service.

4. Apple Music Integration

TrackHUD connects to your Apple Music account to observe currently playing music and build song statistics. This requires permission to access your media library. Your listening history is only analyzed on your device and is not shared externally.

5. In-App Purchases

TrackHUD offers in-app purchases processed by the Apple App Store and/or Google Play. We do not store or handle your payment information directly. All transactions are managed by the respective platform, subject to their terms and privacy policies.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, please visit our Support page.
